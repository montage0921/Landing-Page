Blackpink (Hangul: 블랙핑크), stylized as BLACKPINK or BLΛƆKPIИK, is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment and the first girl group to debut under the same entertainment company seven years after 2NE1. Blackpink's fans are called "Blink", a name which means the fans start with Blackpink and end with Blackpink.
